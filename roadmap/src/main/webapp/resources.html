Resume Help

Experience in the field is an important step when pursuing a career Computer Science. In order to successfully apply to these opportunities, you'll need a strong resume showcasing your skills! Here are some tips on how you can build your resume:

Formatting Tips:

  • Use 1/2" - 1" margins and keep document to one page
  • Choose one font style throughout document and avoid script fonts.
  • Font size should be no less than 10pt.
  • Consistency is key - pay attention to all small formatting details
  • The final document should be in PDF-format, with your name in the PDF title

Including Contact Information:

  • Include your name in a large font, at the top of the page.
  • Include a mailing address where you will spend your time if you get the internship.
  • Include an email adress, university adresses are best!
  • Include your phone number, make sure your voicemail is set-up and professional.

Skill Set Tips:

  • Include technical skills, such as programming languages, computing platforms, lab equipment, and or techniques, etc.
  • Include language skills: highlight additional languages you can speak confidently.
  • Include professional skills, such as certifications, soft-skills mentioned in the job description, etc.

Final Tips:

  • Make sure to proofread! Check spelling, grammar, dates, etc.
  • Print your resume! This helps check hardcopy formatting.
  • Stay organized! Save PDF and document formats of your resume.
